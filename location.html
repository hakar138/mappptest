How Peer-to-Peer Location Sharing Works

Our Solar Hat technology uses WebRTC peer-to-peer networking to enable location sharing without a central database:

No Central Database

Location data is never stored on any server. It's shared directly between devices.

Real-Time Updates

See locations update in real-time as people move around campus.

Complete Privacy

You control exactly who can see your location at any time.

Works Offline

Devices connect directly when in range, no internet required.
